What is My-Team?

This website is a tool for coaches and players alike to stay updated on their team. From uploading team files and displaying the team roster in detail, this tool is perfect for managing your team.

How do I use My-Team?

If you are a coach, the first thing you want to do is signup as a coach. After inputting the information requested, your team has just been created. From here, you can let your players know to come to the website and signup, selecting your team, and again providing the requested information. It's as simple as that.
